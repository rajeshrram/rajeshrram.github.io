Today i have used one Firefox Add-on ,that's an amazing tool. Its like our Gmail,Yahoo and Hotmail Signature option .If you have this add-on, you don’t need to set all your mailboxes' signature option.Insert the signature in one place and use it for all your mailboxes .It separates our Personal and Business Signatures. It supports Rich Text Editor (this option is not in Gmail).
